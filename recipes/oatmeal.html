Protein powder oatmeal

Description

This protein oatmeal is thick, creamy, and packs in over 30 grams of protein per serving! Easy to customize and makes a healthy and filling breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup protein powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk any milk works
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Add the oats, water, milk, and salt to a small saucepan or microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Bring the oats to a boil then once boiling, reduce to a simmer. Let the oats simmer until they are nice and thick.
  3. Remove the oatmeal off the heat and whisk in the protein powder.
  4. Add toppings and enjoy immediately.
